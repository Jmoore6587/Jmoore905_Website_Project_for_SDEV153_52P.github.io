Warhammer 40k

A Quick Primer for the Casually Interested

The Warhammer 40k universe is vast and populated with a variety of races who have whole host of believes. Now existing in the same universe, but not in the same space is what is refered to as the Warp. The Warp is basically a devine realm of energy that that embodies all the extra worldly items in the world.
